Safety Tips

Celebrate safely by using firecrackers in open spaces and keeping water or sand nearby in case of emergencies. Ensure diyas and candles are placed away from flammable materials and never leave them unattended. Choose eco-friendly ways to celebrate, protecting pets and the environment from loud noises and pollution.
